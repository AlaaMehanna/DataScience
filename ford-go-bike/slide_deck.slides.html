This exploration and visualization study mainly focuses on finding the bike usage pattern and customer habit or routine, specifically how user type, time, gender, and age impacts usage of the system.

How does the average trip duration vary in weekdays between customers and subscribers?

The subscribers tend to have really shorter duration trips on average when compared to casual customers, especially on work days (Mon-Fri) but on weekends they tend to have slightly longer durations
